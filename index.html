Location of Bhagwal, Gujrat

Bhagwal is a village and Union Council in Kharian Tehsil, Gujrat District, Punjab, Pakistan. It serves as a rural hub in northern Punjab, connected to nearby villages such as Guliana, Golra Hashim, Kotla Haji Shah, Jhantla, Shorian, Dham Dhal Gujjar, Lammay Chak, Bakhtawar, and Jand Sharif.
